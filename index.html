My Trip to Disneyland

The Road Trip

The road trip from my house in Charotte, NC to Disneyland in Orlando, FL was about 525 miles and took about 7 hours and 30 minutes by car. Everyone that went was my mom, my two brothers, and myself.


My Favorite Ride

Avatar Flight of Passage

The only downside was how long the line was. It took over an hour to get through. However, even this wasn't that bad because this whole area was modelled after the movie. There were a lot of cool props to look at while you wait in line, including a full size Avatar.

My favorite ride at the park was the Flight of Passage inspired by the moive Avatar. This ride is a 3D immersion experience that simulates riding one of the fictional dragon type creatures from movie called a Banshee. This was my favoirte ride I have ever been on at any amusement park.
